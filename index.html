PLACRO

Macro based meal planning

Welcome to Placro

When you're building your meal plan to support your workout programme, you want to make sure that your fueling your body right to reach your goals. Just by entering a few deatils Placro will not only give you a clear overview of what your macros are but also help you choose from a wide range of recipes to build a meal plan tailored to you.

A few details to get started

First up, you need to enter a few simple details to get started. By entering these stats, placro will have the basis of what it needs to generate your target numbers. Next we'll check what goal you're working towards; are you cutting, maintaining, or bulking. That's it - you can then start building your meal plan while placro keeps a track of how much you've 'spent'. You can even save your choices to your browser so they're available next time you come back to the site. Simple!

What is your goal?

Now that you've got your stats down, you need to choose your goal right now. This isn't fixed and you can adjust this at any time.

Selecting your goal impacts the mix of macros you need to build your daily calorie target as well as the calorie target itself.

About

What's behind Placro

The science behind the numbers in Placro are based on the programme developed in Bigger Leaner Stronger by Mike Matthews. The principle is based on building muscle by giving the body the right fuel. By planning your meals using macro nutrients you are building a plan that maximises your daily calorie allowance to hit your goals.

What are Macros anyway?

Macro nutrients are listed below:

  • Protein
  • Carbohydrate
  • Fat

The key thing to note is this isn't designed to be a fad style diet overly emphasising or exluding any particular group. In fact its the opposite and designed to help you eat right based on your goals.

What Goals does this actually help with?

Typically in body building you would aim to achieve one of 3 goals with your diet:

  • Bulk - Eat to gain body / muscle mass to support your training plan
  • Maintain - Your meal plan is built to help you maintain your current body weight
  • Cut - For when you want to move towards a more lean body mass, cutting your body fat

By selecting your goal as part of your stats using Plecro, your target daily calorie intake and macro nutrient mix, are adjusted to deliver on your goal.
